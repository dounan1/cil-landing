CIL (Communication Intelligence Lab) is an AI-powered EdTech solution designed to provide a standardized, data-driven platform for mastering critical thinking and communication. Beyond boosting student academic performance, CIL empowers educators to teach more effectively while giving administrators real-time visibility into classroom progress for smarter resource management.
Already active in over a dozen schools across North America and Asia, CIL delivers proven impact:
Immediate Gains: A 20-30% boost in standardized test scores.
Mid-term Success: Students excel in competitive speech and debate.
Long-term Vision: We anticipate a transformative impact on both holistic soft skills and rigorous IB results.
